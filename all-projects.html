Character Design

December 2024

Riot
Type: Playable Character
Description: A rebellious force of nature, Riot lives to break rules and smash expectations. With her neon-dyed hair, spiked jacket, and custom guitar-turned-weapon, she’s a walking anthem of chaos. Riot’s arsenal includes soundwave attacks that stagger enemies, amplified by her signature power chords. Whether she’s shredding solos to rally allies or using raw energy to blast foes, her style is as loud as her impact. Abilities:
Power Chord: Send shockwaves through enemies with a bone-rattling riff. Sonic Boom: Unleash a devastating AoE blast that disorients and damages. Amped Up: Buffs team movement and attack speed with a high-octane solo. Encore: Revive downed allies with a defiant, crowd-pleasing finale. Unpredictable, unstoppable, and unapologetically loud—Riot brings the noise, both on the battlefield and off.

Game Lobby

December 2024

Type: Game Lobby Environment
Description: Step into the Neon Nexus, a vibrant, engaging hub, where players gather before diving into the action. Bathed in dynamic neon lighting and pulsating with an ambient synthwave soundtrack, the lobby feels alive with energy. Giant holographic screens display leaderboards and match updates, while interactive kiosks let players customize loadouts, check stats, or socialize.

Progression overview: Track your journey to greatness with ease! The lobby's stat trackers provide a clear overview of your progression, from match history to achievements—keeping you motivated to climb even higher."

Interactive Elements: Tap into vending machines for aesthetic boosts, or explore hidden details like graffiti Easter eggs.
More than a waiting area: The Neon Nexus Lobby sets the stage for the battles ahead, blending style and functionality to immerse players in the game world from the moment they log in.
