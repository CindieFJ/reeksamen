WELCOME

My name is Cindie Jønsson and I’m a passionate multimedia designer student, with a strong focus on game design.

I thrive on every stage of the, creative process from initial concept development and visual design to final implementation.

My work is driven by a love for storytelling and interactive media, aiming to create experiences that brings unique characters and worlds to life. Throughout my studies, I have developed the ability to create experience designs through various projects, honing my skills in industry-leading software.

In my portfolio, you will find examples of character designs , game assets , game menus and and 3D experiences.


Feel free to explore and enjoy!

Games

I’m passionate about developing casual and mobile games that are fun, accessible, and engaging. I enjoy creating vibrant characters, intuitive mechanics, and rewarding gameplay loops. My thing? - playful designs, and polished game menus — crafted to deliver experiences that captivate players of all ages.

Design

Design plays a crucial role in my creative process. I use tools like Illustrator and Figma to craft intuitive interfaces and engaging visuals, ensuring a seamless user experience. For 3D projects, Blender and construct helps bring my ideas to life with immersive environments and dynamic assets, delivering designs that are both functional and visually compelling.
